# AquaBill: Water Utility Management System

## 1. Introduction
AquaBill is a complete digital solution designed to simplify and automate the day-to-day operations of water utility providers. It replaces manual paperwork by centralizing customer records, meter readings, billing, and financial tracking into one easy-to-use, secure platform.

## 2. Department Access & User Roles
To keep your data secure and organized, the system is divided into specific departments. Staff members are given accounts that only allow them to see the tools and information necessary for their specific job:

- **Administration**: Manages the overall system. This team creates staff accounts, assigns roles, and sets up global company rules (such as standard connection fees or maintenance charges).
- **Finance**: Oversees the overall financial health of the organization, tracking total revenue and viewing high-level financial reports.
- **Ledger / Accounting**: Handles day-to-day financial transactions. They record customer payments, track outstanding balances, and ensure the books are balanced.
- **Human Resources (HR)**: Manages internal employee records and staff details.
- **Customer Care**: The frontline team that assists consumers, updates customer contact information, and answers account inquiries.

## 3. Core Features & Capabilities

### 3.1. Customer Management
- **Centralized Records**: Keep all consumer details safely in one place.
- **Complete Profiles**: Quickly view a customer's entire history, their assigned water meters, and their current account balance.
- **Custom Charges**: Easily apply specific recurring or one-time fees (like a late fee or repair charge) to individual customer accounts.

### 3.2. Meters & Geographic Zones
- **Infrastructure Tracking**: Maintain a clear, digital inventory of all physical water meters.
- **Zone Organization**: Group customers and meters by geographic area (Zones). This makes sending staff out for the monthly meter reading process much faster and more efficient.

### 3.3. Meter Reading Process
- **Simple Data Entry**: Staff can easily input the latest numbers directly from a customer's water meter.
- **Automated Math**: The system automatically calculates exactly how much water was used by comparing the new reading to the previous month's reading, eliminating manual math errors.

### 3.4. Automated Billing Engine
- **Effortless Invoicing**: At the push of a button, the system generates accurate bills based on the water used, your current pricing structure (Tariffs), and any extra service charges.
- **Overdue Tracking**: Automatically flags unpaid bills so your team knows exactly which accounts need payment follow-ups.
- **Professional Printing**: Print high-quality, professional invoices for individual customers, or use the bulk-printing tool to print hundreds of bills at once for postal mail distribution.

### 3.5. Payment Processing
- **Accurate Tracking**: Easily record when and how a customer pays their bill.
- **Real-Time Updates**: As soon as a payment is entered into the system, the customer's account balance drops and the organization's revenue totals are updated instantly.

### 3.6. Reports & Business Insights
- **Revenue Tracking**: View clear charts showing income over time, helping management understand financial trends and cash flow.
- **Water Usage Analytics**: Track how much water is being consumed across different zones to spot trends, predict future community demand, or identify potential large-scale leaks in your infrastructure.

## 4. Key Benefits for Your Organization
- **Reduced Errors**: Automated math means fewer billing mistakes and fewer customer complaints.
- **Massive Time Savings**: Tasks that used to take days (like calculating and formatting hundreds of bills) are now completed in minutes.
- **Better Customer Service**: Customer care staff have immediate access to accurate, up-to-date account histories, leading to faster and friendlier issue resolution.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Real-time reporting gives your management team the clear, accurate insights needed to make smart business choices.
